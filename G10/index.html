About this website

This website is about Chinese food.

Chinese food

Before about 5000 years, Chinese food appeared. Now, it includes all the food from different regions in China. Because of its characteristics: long history, excellent feats, various category, numerous genres and unique style, Chinese food is famous all around the world. In this website, it is divided to five flavors.

Five Flavors

There are five flavors: sour, sweet, bitter, spicy and salty.
